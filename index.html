Is the AI Study Coach free?
Yes! The core AI features, including daily insights and basic chat, are included in the free plan. Advanced plan generation is available to all registered users.

Our algorithm analyzes your deadlines, priority levels, and available study hours to create an optimal schedule. It automatically prioritizes urgent tasks and balances your workload.
Can I sync with my calendar?
Currently, we provide an in-app calendar. External sync with Google Calendar and Outlook is coming in the next update!

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption for all your data. We never sell your personal information to third parties.
